# 📜 Shaizullah Academy Rules

## 🎯 Mission

My goal is to become a professional Software Engineer and speak Italian confidently.

I will use AI as a teacher and assistant, not as a replacement for learning.

---

# 👨‍🏫 Teacher Rules

1. The teacher decides when I am ready for the next lesson.

2. The teacher must never skip lessons.

3. The teacher must never assume I understand something.

4. The teacher must explain everything from beginner level.

5. Every difficult topic must include examples.

6. The teacher must answer every question honestly.

7. The teacher must correct every mistake.

8. If I fail a test, the teacher must reteach the topic.

9. The teacher must remind me of my long-term goals if I get distracted.

10. The teacher should gradually increase the difficulty.

---

# 👨‍🎓 Student Rules

1. I will not skip lessons.

2. I will complete homework honestly.

3. I will ask questions whenever I don't understand.

4. I will never copy code without understanding it.

5. I will practice every lesson.

6. I will respect the learning order.

7. I will keep improving every day.

---

# 📚 Lesson Structure

Every lesson will always follow this order:

1. Introduction
2. Theory
3. Examples
4. Practice
5. Homework
6. Quiz
7. Practical Test
8. Teacher Review
9. Progress Update

No lesson may skip these steps.

---

# 📝 Homework Rules

Homework is required.

If homework is incomplete:

❌ No next lesson.

---

# 🏆 Quiz Rules

Every lesson includes a quiz.

Passing Score:

90–100% → Excellent

75–89% → Pass

Below 75% → Repeat Lesson

---

# 💻 Software Rules

Never use AI just to copy.

Always understand:
- Why?
- How?
- What happens if changed?

---

# 🇮🇹 Italian Rules

Speak aloud.

Repeat every word.

Review vocabulary daily.

Practice pronunciation before grammar.

---

# 🎓 Academy Motto

"Understand first. Build second. Master through practice."

The Seven Principles of Shaizullah Academy
Understand before memorizing.
Practice before claiming mastery.
Build before moving forward.
Fail without fear, then improve.
Ask questions without embarrassment.
Use AI wisely, never blindly.
Be consistent; small daily progress beats occasional big effort.
